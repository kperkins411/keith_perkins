Optical Head-Mounted Displays for Laser Safety Eyewear, U.S. Patent Number 10,690,918. An augmented Reality application that allows laser operators to see a beam while wearing safety glasses designed to completely filter the beam. (NASA Langley collaboration)
Laser safety glasses are designed to filter a particular color laser beam in order to protect user vision. Unfortunately this also renders the beam invisible which makes it difficult to align a beam with it's target. This patent proposes a system where the beam is rendered on an Augmented Reality (AR) headset in a color which is visible through the laser safety glasses.
